— About us

Salt Lake's fitness home

Muscle Up Gym is a well-equipped fitness centre in Salt Lake, Kolkata, with two branches and a reputation for friendly, supportive trainers.

Who we are

Muscle Up Gym serves the Salt Lake (Bidhannagar) community with two convenient branches — one in Sector 1 near the swimming pool, and one in Sector 3 near IB Block Park.

Members describe the gym as clean, spacious and well-maintained, with cooperative, knowledgeable trainers and a supportive atmosphere. Whether your goal is losing weight, building muscle, getting stage-ready or simply staying active, there's a program and a coach here for you.

We keep our doors open every day of the week, from early morning to late evening, so your training fits around your life — not the other way around.

Approach

Friendly, hands-on coaching that meets you at your level and helps you progress safely.

Community

A welcoming, supportive environment where members and trainers push each other to improve.

Access

Open Mon–Sun, 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM, across two Salt Lake branches with parking.
